1. Information We Collect

When you use FitBae, we may collect the following categories of information:

a. Information you provide directly

  • Profile information: name, age, height, weight.
  • Health-related inputs: menstrual cycle details, preferences, and habits.

b. Information from Apple HealthKit (with your consent)

  • Activity (steps, workouts, calories, movement, sleep, standing time).
  • Other health data you allow the App to access.

c. Automatically collected data

  • Basic app usage statistics (e.g., streak counts, challenge completions).
  • No advertising identifiers, tracking, or third-party analytics are used.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal and health data solely to:

  • Personalize your challenges and recommendations.
  • Track your progress (weight changes, daily activity, streaks).
  • Provide insights related to your menstrual cycle and fitness.

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data.
  • Share your information with advertisers.
  • Use your data for tracking across other apps or websites.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You choose to provide health and fitness data.
  • Legitimate interests: To operate and improve the App in a way that benefits users.
  • Legal obligations: To comply with applicable laws and App Store policies.

4. Data Retention

Your personal and health data is stored locally on your device.

If you enable iCloud backup, Apple securely manages your backup.

We retain your information only as long as you use the App. Deleting the App or its data from your device removes all stored information.

  • Port your data (export manually, where possible).
